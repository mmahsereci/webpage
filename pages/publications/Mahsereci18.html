Probabilistic Approaches to Stochastic Optimization
In my PhD thesis I introduce novel concepts to stochastic optimization for neural network training. These include probabilistic line searches, probabilistic Wolfe conditions, noise-reduced first and second order search directions via Kalman filtering, generalization and overfitting. Some of the content is not published elsewhere for no particular reason, so please check it out.
M. Mahsereci PhD thesis 2018.
